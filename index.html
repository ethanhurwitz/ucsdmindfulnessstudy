Also, please make sure to fullscreen your browser window. You can do this by clicking on the green button on the top left part of your window on a Mac computer, or the middle square on the top left if you are on Windows.

At the beginning of each, you will be asked to input an ID number. Yours is: $("p").html(randCode).
Please copy/paste this code into the entry box at the beginning of each part. When you are done with a specific part, you can close the new window it created, go back to this page, and click on a new link. Once you have completed all the parts from the links below, the study is over!
